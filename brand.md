## Tone and voice

### Words to use

Human, friendly, encouraging. Second person ("you"). Contractions are fine. Describe cooks as real people with stories — Auntie Rose, Katong heritage, HDB collection. Occasion-first: Hari Raya, CNY, family gatherings.

### Words to avoid

"Utilize", "leverage", "synergy", phase numbers, dev jargon, "demo", "stub", "parity". Don't sound like a backend scaffold or internal sprint doc.

### Voice example

> Order Auntie Rose's buah keluak for your Hari Raya open house. Pick a collection slot, pay with PayNow, and we'll share her HDB address two hours before you arrive.

## Usage dos and don'ts

**Do:**

- Use shadcn CSS variables (`bg-background`, `text-foreground`, `bg-primary`, `text-muted-foreground`) everywhere.
- Use `bg-brand-bg` for hero sections.
- Use `font-mono tabular-nums` for prices and order references.
- Keep cook portal separate from customer nav.

**Don't:**

- Hardcode the old jade green (`#1D9E75`) — primary is now coral (`#D96C4A`).
- Show dev switcher, localhost links, or phase labels in customer UI.
- Mix more than one gradient per screen.
